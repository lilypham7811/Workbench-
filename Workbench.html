Alerts

How alerts work here. When a timer ends you get a sound, an on-screen flash, and — if you allow it below — a notification banner, all while Workbench is open. iPhone doesn't let any web app trigger vibration, and it pauses timers once the screen locks or you leave the app, so keep Workbench open during a focus block.

Real screen-time limits

Workbench can't see how long you've spent in other apps — only Apple's own Screen Time has that access. For a true phone-wide stop alert, pair Workbench with Settings → Screen Time → App Limits on the apps that distract you, and turn on a Focus mode while you work.
